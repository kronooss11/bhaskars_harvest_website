Welcome to Bhasker’s Harvest

At Bhasker’s Harvest, we believe in cultivating a world where tradition and sustainability come together to create pure, organic products. Inspired by the visionary Bhasker Save, known as the ‘Gandhi of Organic Farming,’ we are committed to supporting small-scale farmers who practice sustainable and traditional farming methods. Our mission is to bring the finest cold-pressed oils, organic grains, and spices to your table, nurturing both you and the earth.

Our Story

Named after Bhasker Save, a pioneer in organic farming, Bhasker’s Harvest was born out of a desire to promote natural agricultural practices and make them accessible to conscious consumers. Our journey began with the goal of connecting small-scale farmers with people who value purity, sustainability, and quality. Every product we offer is a testament to the care and dedication of the farmers who work tirelessly to grow food in harmony with nature.

  • 100% Organic
  • 500+ Farms
  • 60% Higher Nutrient Retention
  • 20+ yrs Legacy in Organic Farming

Bhasker’s Harvest exists to bridge the gap between traditional farming and modern consumers. We are committed to preserving age-old organic farming methods and making high-quality, sustainably grown food accessible. Every product we deliver carries the essence of the fields, nurtured by ethical practices and a deep respect for the earth.

To become a leading force in promoting sustainable, organic farming practices that empower farmers, nurture the earth, and provide wholesome, nutrient-rich food to communities across the world. We envision a future where traditional agriculture is valued, and natural food becomes the first choice for all.

Our mission is to deliver premium-quality, cold-pressed oils, organic grains, and spices that are sourced from small-scale farmers practicing organic agriculture. We aim to reconnect consumers with nature, fostering a deeper understanding of where their food comes from and promoting healthy, ethical food consumption.

Sustainability: We are dedicated to eco-friendly farming that protects and preserves the environment.
Integrity: Transparency and fairness define our relationships with farmers, partners, and customers.
Support for Farmers: We work closely with small-scale farmers to ensure they receive fair compensation and recognition for their hard work.
Health & Wellness: Every product we offer is crafted with care, ensuring maximum health benefits and natural goodness for our customers.

Delivery Process

step 01

Sourcing from Organic Farms

We partner with small-scale farmers who use time-honored organic farming methods. Every grain, spice, and drop of oil is grown and harvested sustainably, ensuring the highest quality.

step 02

Meticulous Quality Control

Once harvested, our products undergo rigorous quality checks to maintain purity and freshness. Only the finest organic produce passes through to the next stage, ensuring exceptional quality for our customers.

step 03

Packaging with Care

Our products are carefully packaged using eco-friendly materials that preserve their natural freshness. Each item is sealed to maintain its nutrients and aroma until it reaches your doorstep.

step 04

Delivered to Your Home

From our farms to your home, our efficient delivery network ensures your products arrive promptly and in perfect condition, ready to nourish you and your family.
